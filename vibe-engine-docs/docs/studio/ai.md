# Neural Copilot

The **Neural Copilot** is the intelligent brain of the VibeEngine Studio. It's a built-in AI assistant that understands your scene, your components, and your goals.

## AI-Native Development

Unlike traditional autocomplete, the Neural Copilot has full context of the **VibeEngine ECS**. It doesn't just suggest code; it understands how everything in your scene works together.

### Key Abilities

- **Scene Insight**: Ask the AI questions about your scene: "Why is the bridge entity not casting shadows?"
- **Logic Autocomplete**: The AI helps you write scripts by understanding the surrounding components.
- **Auto-Serialization**: The AI can generate or modify scene JSON files to build complex layouts autonomously.
- **Debugging**: Paste error logs, and the AI will pinpoint the exact entity and component causing the issue.

## Using the Copilot

Open the Neural Copilot panel by clicking the **Brain Icon** (🧠) in the bottom-right corner.

### Common Prompt Examples

- "Add a player entity with a first-person camera and movement scripts."
- "Optimize the lighting settings for a night scene."
- "Explain the lifecycle of the `HealthComponent` I just created."

## Future of AI Integration

We are working on bringing more advanced features to the Neural Copilot:

- **Scene Generation from Text**: "Build a procedurally generated forest scene."
- **Autonomous Error Correction**: "Detect and fix all collision overlapping in the current level."
